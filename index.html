Frequently asked questions.

Does StadiumAmp replace scorekeeping apps like GameChanger?

No. StadiumAmp focuses solely on game-day audio and walk-up music. You can keep using your existing scorekeeping app alongside it.

What devices does it work on?

Any modern browser on phone, tablet, or laptop. For best results, use a recent iOS or Android device with a stable Bluetooth or AUX connection to your speakers.

Do I need Wi-Fi at the field?

StadiumAmp is built as a Progressive Web App and can retain key data offline. We still recommend testing your setup before game day.

Can I use songs from Spotify or Apple Music?

StadiumAmp supports uploaded audio files and YouTube where allowed. Streaming service integrations may not be available due to licensing and API constraints.
